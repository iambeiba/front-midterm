My Education Journey

42 "Ak Niet" Gymnasium

In 42 school I studied for 7 years. Starting from 0 till 6 grade. This school gave me basic knowledges and I was A student all 7 years. In this school I had a lot of good and bad experiences.

KTL/BIL

Then, I enrolled in KTL, why KTL?, I don't know. My parents just wanted to change something and then, I passed exam to KTL and find new friends and new abishkas. Those 5 years were something incredible and now I have 18 brothers all over the KZ.

SDU University

After, KTL I wanted to enroll in NU, but my NUET score was low, that is why I could not enroll there. However, I didn't give up and enrolled in SDU. If be honest, I never complained about SDU, because it gave me new friends and new opportunities that I would not had in NU.
